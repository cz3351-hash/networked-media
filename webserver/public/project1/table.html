Table




My table has two shelves on the top
I store some food,
makeup,
photos,
notebooks,
playbills,
stuffed animals,
and my ardurino kit on my shelves;



My table also has two drawers below it:
The first has my cameras,

phone cases,
mails,

single use forks, spoons and chopsticks,
keys,

passport and id,

a hard disk,
and maybe some more. . .






The second is just some papers.
